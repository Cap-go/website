## What does the main page shows?

First, let's take a look at the main page:
<figure><img src="/main-page.webp" alt="Main page screenshot" /><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Let's Démarrer at the beginning. The first thing you see is the **stats**. It contains Application-specific Statistiques. Those Mises à jour depend on the Utilisation of the Application

The second thing you see is the list of all Disponible apps. This will change depending on how many apps have you created. You can click on each Application to see more details À propos it.

The third thing you see is the **Clés API** button. This button will take you to the [Clés API](/docs/webapp/api-keys/) page.

The Suivant thing you see is the **Test Capgo** button. This button will change depending on your first and second name and it will take you to the [Paramètres](/docs/webapp/Paramètres/) or Support page.

Lastly, you see a button to Ajouter another Application. This button will take you to a page where you can Ajouter a Nouveau Application to capgo.
