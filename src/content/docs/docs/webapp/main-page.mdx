## What does the main page shows?

First let's take a look at the main page:
<figure><img src="/main-page.png" alt="" /><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Let's start at the begining. The first thing you see is the **stats**. It contains app specific statistics. Those update depending on the usage of the app

The second thing you see is the list of all avalible apps. This will change depending on how many apps have you created. You can click on each app to see more details about it.

The third thing you see is the **API keys** button. This button will take you to the [API keys](TODO) page.

The next thing you see is the **test Capgo** button. This button will  change depending on your first and second name and it will take you to the [settings](TODO) or support page.

Lastly you see a butting to add another app. This button will take you to the [create app](TODO) page.
